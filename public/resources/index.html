Vtubing resources

I'll include where to find various resources, and some of my favourite asset creators!

Make sure to read the terms of use before downloading anything from one of these sites.

Asset websites

  • BOOTH - JP site, lots of free and paid assets
  • nizima - JP site, you can preview Live2D models right in the browser!
  • Ko-fi - lots of cute shops, I'll write how to search on Ko-fi later
  • OKUMONO - free to use graphics you can use in layouts
  • AOmaterial - another free graphics site
  • Twitter can be sometimes useful.
  • You can find paid assets on Etsy, if you have money to spare.

Software

I'll add more if I find any.

Streaming software:

OBS - free and open source, lots of plugins that you can add to it, tons of tutorials out there, and performs better certain other software

Overlay widgets and chatbot:

StreamElements - also plenty of cute user-made assets

VTubing:

VTube Studio - the go-to software

VBridger - more advanced tracking for VTube Studio

Cool/fun stuff and gimmicks

(I haven't tested all of these)

Spud Tablet - as well as Spud Controller and Spud Keyboard and Mouse, tracks your mouse and controller movements and animates them

Nyarupad - like Spud Controller

KBonk - interactive throwable items

Concert overlay - interactive concert audience

Heartrate - good for horror/workout streams

Scream X Stream - show everyone how loud you're screaming without those audio settings

VTS Sound Effect Tool - play a sound effect based on your VTS parameters
